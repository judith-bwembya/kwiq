About Us

Our Journey

KWIQ began its operations under the SMICS banner in 2015 and rebranded to Kwiq Group in 2020. Throughout our journey our vision has not altered – to be the most expeditious supplier of quality products and distribution services across the global market from Africa. Today, we transcend borders with exceptional TRANSPORT, LOGISTICS, PETROLEUM and COMMODITIY TRADING services to a variety of clients spanning government, public and private institutions. The company’s diversity combined with a strong capital base and robust management framework has led to our entrance into the Northern and Southern African markets.

Whatever you need, delivered whenever you need it – go Kwiq.
